INASKASEM

Hello, I’m Inas— I propably should write it as 'Ines' the way I pronounce it— I guess it's too late now!

I'm finally a graphic designer based in Ottawa. My story began when I graphically sketched human hair and realized a little graphic designer was hidden in me. When I grew up, graphic design wasn't an option, so I ended up getting a master's degree in TESOL— very weird, I know, but it worked in my favor later in my journey.

We hear people saying, "it takes a war to go after one's passion," but it was pretty literal in my case. When the war started in Syria, I had to leave my home country and immigrate to Canada. When I landed in Ottawa, I looked at the bright side of it and thought to myself: this is my clue, I can study graphic design, and I won't have to learn the language— thanks to the master's degree that I thought I'll never appreciate!

Here at Algonquin College, I was taught by top professors who helped refine my skills and develop my talent. I'm excited to show you what I've learned so far.

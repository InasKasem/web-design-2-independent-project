Logo Rebranding

In this project we had to think creatively, conduct an extensive market research and develope a creative concept that meets the needs of the client presently and in the future. I learned how to work through the stages of creating a logo redesign to produce a logo within 2 colour parameters that can be used within any medium and size.

Stationary Package

Designed and presented a stationery package for the Jad Immigration Services. The stationary submission included a letterhead, a business card, an email signature and a business-sized envelope.

Sustainable Packaging

As packaging designers we had to make choices that help lessen our consumer footprint. My goal was to work with alternative materials, while proofing that sustainable packaging shouldn't always look like plain pizza boxes! I have a love-hate relationship with Oreo (only because of the plastic tray that comes with it) so I chose it for my client.
